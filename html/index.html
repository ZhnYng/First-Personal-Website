About Myself

Values

Honesty, Humility, and Perseverance are my 3 core values. Honesty is important as it makes one trustworthy and reliable.

Humility is defined as the quality of having a modest or low view of one's importance. To be humble entails the ability to empathise with others, to be conscious of one's surrounding and someone else's feelings.

Persevering through adversity and arduous times, not succumbing to difficulty is an important value everyone should have. We should embrace difficulty, approach it with a growth mindset.

Personality

I am tenacious, industrious, and goal-oriented. Tenacity, the quality or fact of being very determined, determines how one reacts to challenges. Personally, I enjoy solving challenges as overcoming them gives me a great sense of accomplishment and I get to learn something new.

I try to complete tasks to the best of my ability, as quickly as I can which prepares me for unforseen circumstances. It has also helped me, rarely finding myself behind time on assignments. I am goal-oriented, always trying to complete tasks as efficiently and accurately as possible.

This allows me to be focused on the tasks at hand, not being distracted by other things. I aim to not only complete my goals, but to excel in them. As the old adage says, "Shoot for the moon. Even if you miss, you'll land among the stars." Even if I don't reach my goals, I would still be among the best.

Interest

I am interested to learn about new technologies, whether it is hardware such as phones or software such as new programming languages, or programming projects, with a recent one called PyScript, which allows one to run python code directly in html.

I also have interest in machine learning, how it works, and its seemingly limitless ability to learn. Its ability to enchance human life, making the world a safer place with models capable of identifying fake news, to creating art with word descriptions as input from the user.
